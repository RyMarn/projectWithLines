Computer Science

Major Description:

The computer science major program is a 120-credit hour degree program in the Faculty of Science that provides a solid background for a career in computer science. Major students are eligible for the co-op program and specialization areas.To enter the program students must have completed 24 credit hours with a minimum 2.0 GPA a minimum grade of C+ in COMP 1020, a C+ in both MATH 1300 and MATH 1500 as well as a C in MATH 1700.A minimum GPA of 2.0 must be maintained to remain in the program.
